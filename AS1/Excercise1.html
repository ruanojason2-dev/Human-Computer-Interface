Excercise 1: Noticing the Design of Everyday Things

Task: The purpose of this excersie is to identify 3 usability problems on the things or objects that we use on a daily basis
and propose a resolution to those problems.

Object: Door

This door is a door that I use with frequency because it is at my house. This door was either poorly designed or it's just bad work.
Whichever the case, one can infer that it's poorly designed for 2 main reasons. Here's why:

  • It has a missing handle. This makes it difficult for the user to lock the door from the inside
  • The less obvious one is that the door doesn't quite close naturally. One needs to push the door inward (assuming your locking it from outside
    and due to the missing handle (inside) and this force that needs to be made to lock the door, makes it virtually impossible for the door to lock
    from the inside.
